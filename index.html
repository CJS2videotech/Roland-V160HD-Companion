5. Simple Troubleshooting & Setup Launch

If something isn't working right on-site, review these quick checks before opening the main console:

1. Make sure your laptop is securely connected to physical **HDMI Input 1** on the switcher.

2. Verify the laptop signal status shows **Connected** (Step 2 check).

3. Tap **PGM 1** (Step 3 check) to make the laptop feed live on the program bus.

Gain-Staging Golden Rules

  • Mute Unused Analog Inputs: Keep Mic 1/Mic 2 muted if no active microphone is physical patched to block baseline pre-amp buzz.
  • Target Computer Output 40% (HDMI): Set laptop volume strictly to 40% if running audio over the HDMI line to protect inputs from pre-mixer digital clipping.
  • DI Box output 95%+: If routing audio via the laptop headphone jack through a DI box, push laptop volume to at least 95% to maintain signal-to-noise strength.
